FleetSync


About FleetSync

Many of our club members use Kenwood Commercial radios that have access to Kenwood's Fleetsync. We use basic Fleetsync at 2400 Baud with ID's occuring at the beginning and end of transmission. In the future we are looking at implimenting Fleetsync status messages. When we do another table will be created on this page containing them. ID's are based on the DTMF lettering of the last 4 characters of a callsign to create a semi-unique value. The Club fleet ID is 100.

MDC1200


About MDC1200

For the few members that run Motorola equipment, our club also uses far superior MDC1200 ID's based on the same system as our FleetSync ID's. The same 4 digit ID's are used in both systems.
